## Delivered controls

The repository contains 20 policies in French, English, and Spanish, each generated as source Markdown, accessible print HTML, and page-numbered PDF. The generator and validator keep version, dates, company information, support details, filenames, and editions consistent.

Registration now requires explicit Terms acceptance, Privacy acknowledgement, and an 18+ confirmation. Marketing remains optional. The backend records document type, version, language, timestamp, IP address, user agent, and revocation time and writes acceptance/revocation audit events.

Authenticated members receive a Privacy Center supporting a structured export of profile, photos, chats, verification details, reports, wallet history, and legal acceptance history. Account deletion requires password verification and explicit confirmation, then starts a cancellable 30-day grace period. Export and deletion lifecycle actions are audited.

## Compliance posture

The policy set establishes GDPR rights for access, rectification, deletion, restriction, portability, objection, consent withdrawal, and applicable automated-decision review; PIPEDA fair-information principles; Malabo Convention privacy principles; international-transfer safeguards including SCC references; processor and subprocessor obligations; KYC and liveness governance; child protection; AML, sanctions, fraud and scam controls; and a transparency-report framework.

This sprint documents future wallet, mobile-money, PawaPay, CinetPay, Pi and crypto behavior without activating or integrating any payment provider and without changing wallet, matchmaking, or marriage business logic.
