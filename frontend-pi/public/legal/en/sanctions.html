Commitment

Nexa does not knowingly provide prohibited services to sanctioned persons, entities, territories or transactions and will comply with sanctions applicable to its operations and providers.

Screening and controls

When risk and law require, Nexa or providers may screen identity and transaction information, ownership and location indicators, investigate matches, block or reject activity and retain evidence.

User representation

Users must not use LYNK to evade sanctions, act for a blocked party or conceal origin, destination, ownership or purpose. False positives may be appealed with reliable evidence.

Changes

Sanctions change quickly. Availability may be restricted without advance notice where necessary. Nothing requires Nexa to perform an act prohibited by applicable law.

Privacy and compliance baseline

Nexa applies data minimization, purpose limitation, accuracy, security, accountability and privacy-by-design. This document is intended to align with applicable GDPR principles, Canada’s PIPEDA fair-information principles, the African Union Malabo Convention and applicable DRC law; mandatory local rights prevail.
